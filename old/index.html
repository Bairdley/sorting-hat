Project Sorting Hat

Easily manage your customer signups by automatically sorting them into the right customer group!

Setup Instructions

Create a custom form field

Create a form field in the Address Form settings that will be used to sort customers into corresponding groups.

List the available values

Once the Address Form field is created, list all available values that a customer can choose.

Create your customer groups

Create the customer groups that correspond with each value you've selected. You can hide categories or even give discounts based on these groups. More info here.

Tell us the field name

In the "Form Field" box below, enter the name of the Address Form field you created so we can find it when customers sign up.

Map your customer groups & values

In the box next to each of your customer group names, type the exact name of the form field value that should sort a customer into that group.

You're all set!

Now anytime your customers sign up, they will be placed in the right group based on their response in your form field.
